About Me

I am a student at the University of Minnesota Duluth pursuing a double major in Financial Markets and Accounting, driven by a passion for understanding how businesses operate and how financial decisions impact markets and communities. Originally from Shakopee, Minnesota, I have always been curious about the intersection of numbers, strategy, and decision-making, which naturally led me to this path. Through my professional experience at Hy-Vee, Inc., I have honed skills in customer relationship management, teamwork, and effective communication, learning the importance of both precision and empathy in a professional setting. Beyond the workplace, I serve as the head coach of a youth lacrosse team, where I apply leadership, organization, and mentorship to help young athletes build confidence, discipline, and teamwork. My accounting internship at CHUM provides me with practical, hands-on experience in payroll management, operational coordination, and financial analysis, further strengthening my technical and analytical abilities. My career goal is to leverage this combination of finance, accounting, and leadership experience to contribute meaningfully in the business world, whether through investment analysis, corporate finance, or financial consulting. On this website, visitors will find insights into my academic journey, professional experience, projects, and personal interests, offering a comprehensive view of who I am, what I value, and the skills I bring to the table. I am committed to continuous learning and growth, and I aim to apply both analytical rigor and creative problem-solving in every professional challenge I take on.
