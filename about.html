A Deeper Dive into my Hobbies

Photography

My love for photography started with my phone camera, capturing moments wherever I went. On my 18th birthday in March, I decided to take my hobby to the next level by investing over $1,000 in a full camera setup. That decision marked the beginning of a more serious and immersive journey into photography. I love finding new ways to enjoy nature, and having a camera has given me the perfect excuse to explore the outdoors while capturing its beauty.

I particularly enjoy doing photoshoots for people and capturing wildlife in moments that feel timeless. My favorite subjects include nature, sports, people, and wildlife, each offering its own unique challenge and reward. I’ve learned to master manual mode on my camera, experimenting with different apertures, shutter speeds, and ISOs to achieve the perfect shot. Every shoot teaches me something new, whether it’s lighting, composition, or patience in waiting for the right moment.

Photography has had a meaningful impact on my life by giving me something I’m truly proud of. It has strengthened my creativity, attention to detail, and appreciation for the world around me. Looking forward, I hope to turn my skills into a way to earn income, selling my photography or offering professional services. More than just a hobby, photography has become a way for me to capture memories, express myself, and share the beauty I see with others.

I first started playing lacrosse when I was 8 years old with the Shakopee Youth Lacrosse Association. From the very beginning, I was drawn to the competitiveness of the sport and the friendships that quickly turned into family. Lacrosse gave me a sense of belonging and excitement that I couldn’t find anywhere else, and it’s been a major part of my life ever since.

Playing as a defenseman has taught me important skills both on and off the field. Over the years, I’ve developed strong communication and leadership abilities, and I now serve as the team secretary with a path toward VP and eventually president. Every game and practice has challenged me to grow in discipline, teamwork, and confidence, while pushing me to be a better player and teammate.

Lacrosse has had a profound impact on my life, shaping how I approach challenges and interact with others. It has strengthened my leadership skills, taught me the value of dedication, and built lasting relationships. Looking ahead, I hope to continue playing in college and further develop both my skills and my love for the game. Lacrosse isn’t just a sport for me, it’s a lifelong passion that continues to shape who I am.
